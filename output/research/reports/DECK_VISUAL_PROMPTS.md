> Use these with Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion, or Adobe Firefly.

## STYLE GUIDE SUMMARY

Apply consistently across all slide backgrounds and generated images:

| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| Primary background | Dark navy `#0A1628` |
| Accent color | Electric blue `#0A84FF` |
| Secondary accent | Warm amber `#F5A623` (use sparingly) |
| Text on dark | Pure white `#FFFFFF` |
| Body text | Soft white `#E8EDF4` |
| Font style | Modern sans-serif (e.g. Inter, Neue Haas Grotesk, or similar) |
| Image feel | Cinematic, photorealistic, premium, editorial |
| What to avoid | Stock photo clichés, handshakes, generic "business people", cartoons |
