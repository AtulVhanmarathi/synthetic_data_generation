## Who is PlaneSense?

PlaneSense is the **second oldest fractional aircraft program in the world**, founded in 1995,
headquartered in Portsmouth, NH. They operate the **largest U.S. commercial fleet of Pilatus
aircraft** — PC-12 turboprops and PC-24 jets — with **600+ employees** across two maintenance
facilities (Portsmouth, NH and Boulder City, NV).

They have been the **launch customer for the Pilatus PC-24 jet** (first delivery February 2018)
and operate exclusively Pilatus aircraft — giving them the deepest OEM relationship of any
commercial operator worldwide.

**Scale indicators (verified from planesense.com):**

- 240+ pilots, 40+ reporting bases
- Access to thousands of airports including short grass and dirt strips
- Average fleet age: 4.5 years
- FAA Diamond Award for Excellence — every year since 2002
- ARGUS Platinum safety status held continuously since 2016

## Top 3 Opportunities — Connect to Calfus

### 1. IOC Intelligence Automation *(Daily operational pain)*
The Integrated Operations Center runs 24/7 with a human team managing crew
scheduling, reservations, weather rerouting, event slot management, catering, and
partner coordination (Jetfly + CaptainJet) — simultaneously, for every flight.
Three new programs in 2025 multiply that coordination load without a corresponding
technology layer.

**Calfus angle:** AI agents for intelligent dispatch, crew-trip matching, and
weather-triggered rerouting. A direct parallel to how AssistIQ was built for
Oracle support — applied to aviation operations.

> *"How are you managing IOC coordination across CobaltPass, Sourcing Solution,
> and Jetfly simultaneously — is that all your existing team?"*
